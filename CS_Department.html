Computer Science Department

About the Department

The Computer Science Department at George Mason University offers an array of degree programs aimed at preparing students for successful careers in various fields of computer science and information technology. Our faculty are highly experienced and dedicated to fostering an environment of innovation, research, and learning.

Our graduate programs provide students with both theoretical and practical knowledge, making them competitive in the rapidly evolving tech industry. Students can pursue several Master of Science degrees that cover a wide range of specializations.

Master of Science Degrees Offered

  • MS in Computer Science
  • MS in Information Security and Assurance
  • MS in Software Engineering
  • MS in Information Systems
